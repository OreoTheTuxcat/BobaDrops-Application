Active Spaceships as of 2025

Active spaceships in late 2025 include the International Space Station (ISS), crewed spacecraft like Soyuz, Shenzhou, and SpaceX's Crew Dragon, and uncrewed probes like Voyager 1 & 2, New Horizons, Parker Solar Probe, and NASA's Artemis-bound Orion spacecraft. There are also active orbital and planetary missions, including asteroid orbiters like Psyche and Hera, and NASA's Hubble Space Telescope and James Webb Space Telescope.
